Local Food

Looking for recommendations for a must-try local cuisine here in the Philippines? Don't you worry 'cause I got your back! Here are my favorite picks of local dishes here in the Philippines! Ready your tummy and feel the greatness of the Filipino cuisine! Tara kain!

Adobo

Adobo is the immersion of cooked food in a stock (or sauce) composed variously of paprika, oregano, salt, garlic, and vinegar to preserve and enhance its flavor.

Menudo

Menudo, also known as ginamay or ginagmay, is a traditional stew from the Philippines made with pork and sliced liver in tomato sauce with carrots and potatoes

Pancit

Pancit is a general term referring to various traditional noodle dishes in Filipino cuisine. There are numerous types of pancit, often named based on the noodles used or ingredients.

Read More

Sisig

Sisig is a Filipino dish made from parts of a pig's face and belly, and chicken liver which is usually seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers.
